Gourmet

Welcome to Gourmet, an app that allows you to keep track of your favorite restaurants. Take a few minutes to learn the ins and outs of how it works, or, if you already know, then jump right in.

Start by clicking the location of a restaurant you've been to before on the map

Fill out the form

See your restaurant appear on the map

Click the bookmark to see a list of your restaurants

Use the filter and sort features to look for restaurants that meet your specifications

Click the navigation icon to locate a specific restaurant on the map

Click the trash icon to delete a restaurant from your list and remove it from the map

Click the navigation compass to find your current location
